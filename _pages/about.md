I conduct research in acoustic metadiffusers and their potential application in orchestra pits in order to overhaul the ensemble conditions of performing musicians. The main emphasis of the current research focuses on the geometry variablity of metadiffusers, and the inherent acoustic properties they exhibit, to prove that these structures can be efficient for controlling sound diffusion and absorption at very low scales compared to traditional means.
