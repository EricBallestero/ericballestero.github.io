Eric Ballestero

Post-Doctoral Researcher in acoustics and acoustic metamaterials

Post-Doctoral researcher at Laboratoire d'Acoustique de l'Université du Mans (LAUM UMR-CNRS 6613), Le Mans, France, I am specialised in passive acoustic metamaterials for controlling wave propagation. I conducted a 3.5-year PhD on metamaterial-inspired ultra-thin acoustic diffusers (i.e., metadiffusers) in order to see how these could be further designed to achieve specific features and how they could be implemented into practical numerical schemes typically used in predicting the acoustics of auditoria. I am currently working on the design of multi-variable acoustic metamaterials in an ANR project, METARoom, for designing deep-subwavelength reconfigurable acoustic treatments for room acoustic purposes.
