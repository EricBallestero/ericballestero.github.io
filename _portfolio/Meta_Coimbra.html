--- title: "Scientific Exhibition" excerpt: "A Quadratic Residue Metadiffuser showcased at the Science Museum of Coimbra, Portugal.
" collection: portfolio --- In February 2020, a Quadratic Residue Metadiffuser (QRM) has been placed at the Science Museum of the University of Coimbra, Portugal, for a free public exhibition on acoustics organized by the European Cooperation in Science & Technology (COST) Action 15125, Design in Noise Reducing Materials and Structures (DENORMS).
